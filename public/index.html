So, how does this work?

It's simple, really. We, or users like you, submit challenges every so often, and you submit photos to them.

Each challenge has a short description. The idea, though, is that the challenge is whatever the challenge's name is. If we have a challenge for, say, Struggle, a user submits a photo corresponding to that word. It could be a photo of a tree growing on an incline, or of an ant carrying a piece of food.

The idea is that it's all open-ended. Really, all you need is your camera and the challenge title.
